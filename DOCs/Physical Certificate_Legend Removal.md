**Documents and Review Process**

1.  **Required Documentation**

    - Scanned copies (front and back) of physical stock certificates, ensuring legibility.

    - A completed Low Price Security Questionnaire (LPSQ).

    - Electronic signature summary (if applicable).

    - Supporting documents (e.g., subscription agreements) showing how the shares were acquired.

2.  **Compliance and Approval Steps**

    - RJLU Compliance reviews all materials and may contact the FA/client for any needed clarifications. LPSQs and supporting documents are saved in our shared drive.

    - Once approved, the Branch Manager reviews all supporting documents and signs the LPSQ.

    - Certificates are then sent to Pershing Custody for final review. Accepted securities are deposited into the client’s account.

    - If restricted, the position appears as **RSTK** in client’s holding.

    - When legend-free shares are issued, RJLU Compliance restricts the account with a note specifying that these shares: Cannot be traded on any U.S. market. Cannot be knowingly sold to any U.S. resident. Cannot be transferred without Branch Manager/Compliance approval.

**Security/Issuer Eligibility of Deposit/:**

Firm’s policy of low price security prohibit deposit of securities/issuers that are either:

1.  Designated prohibited securities

    1.  To check if a security is currently prohibited, please download the latest Prohibited Security Search tool

2.  the current price of common stock of the security issuer less than 50 cents per share (USD)

3.  Assigned to Grey Market or Expert Market Tier

4.  Designated Caveat Emptor

**Compliance Due Diligence Review:**

To proceed with the deposit of restricted low-price securities,

- it is necessary for the client to obtain physical stock certificates from the transfer agent,

- provide documentation supporting the acquisition of these shares.

- Additionally, please ensure the [Low-Price Security Questionnaire](https://raymondjamescan.sharepoint.com/sites/rjnet-ps-rjlu/FormServerTemplates/Forms/All%20Forms.aspx?id=%2Fsites%2Frjnet%2Dps%2Drjlu%2FFormServerTemplates%2FLow%2DPriced%20Security%20Questionnaire%2Epdf&parent=%2Fsites%2Frjnet%2Dps%2Drjlu%2FFormServerTemplates) is fully completed, signed and dated by the client.

For compliance review by RJLU, kindly submit the following documents to <Compliance@RJLU.com>

1.  Scanned copies of both the front and back of the physical stock certificates.

2.  The completed and signed Low Price Security Questionnaire.
