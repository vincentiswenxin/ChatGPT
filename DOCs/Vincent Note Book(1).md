Below is an overview of the **BI Centre of Excellence (BICE)**, along with the schedule for the first half of 2026.

 

**Purpose of BICE Meetings**

The BI Centre of Excellence (BICE) is a monthly forum designed for analysts and engineers from the Enterprise Data team to share technical knowledge, best practices, standards, and guidance with non-IT departments that also develop Power BI reports or have interesting in BI and data-related topics.

The goal is to promote consistency, excellence, and alignment across the company in areas such as data usage, modeling, architecture, governance, and Power BI development.

BICE is intended to be a casual and informal meeting — an open space where we can share knowledge, discuss the monthly topic, exchange ideas, and ask questions. It is a collaborative learning opportunity rather than a formal presentation.

In addition to Enterprise Data presenters, non‑IT teams are also encouraged to present whenever they have something valuable to share — for example, showcasing a meaningful report they developed, a solution they implemented, or a lesson learned that could benefit the broader group. The goal is to create a space where everyone can contribute and learn from each other.

 

**Frequency**

- Once per month

- Last Friday of each month

- **Duration:** up to 1 hour

- **Format:** presentation + Q&A

 

***Note: **If you are no longer interested in joining these sessions, please feel free to decline the invite.*

*You are also welcome to forward this invitation to other members of your team who may benefit from participating.*
